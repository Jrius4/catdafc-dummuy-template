About Catda

In the 2017-18 season, we were crowned the champions of the Nakawa District and thus promoted to the 3rd Division in the Ugandan Football hierarchy; Kampala Regional League.

Our first season in the 3rd Division was well; as we finished runners up.

We are glad with the philosophy of the club which interests and attacks fans from different areas to come and watch the great style of football the boys play. more to that, recently during the Cranes Regional Tours, we had four of our boys called up for the Kampala Select Team.

We are grateful for the support and love from you our dear fans. Thank you. We are the #LufulaBoys.

Our players in Masaza Cup

The Masaza Cup tournament 2019 has seen many of our boys take part with so far three of them recording their names on the score sheet with Kizito Fahad so far with 2goals, Ssenabulya James and Walugembe Derrick each once on the score sheet.

Ssemakula Francis has captained Busiro while Mayanja Ivan and Kasule Brian have also been instrumental to Busiro's good performance this season.
